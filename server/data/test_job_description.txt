
Zoox is a company focused on developing autonomous vehicles, and they are seeking a Machine Learning Engineer Intern for their Simulation team. The intern will work on various teams to create high fidelity synthetic data, develop models for simulating road agent behavior, and improve the fidelity of sensor simulation.

Responsibilities
Extend our simulation pipeline to reproduce dynamic actors accurately in simulation
Research and evaluate various reconstruction algorithms and integrate the most promising of them into our simulation framework
Develop and improve models that realistically simulate the behavior of other road agents like pedestrians and vehicles
Leverage software engineering and machine learning skills to ensure these simulated agents exhibit a wide range of plausible and representative human behaviors
Help create high fidelity synthetic data for training and testing AV AI, as well as real time sensor data for hardware-in-the-loop simulation
Develop 3D ML models and training datasets for 3D sensor simulation using modern machine learning architectures
Collaborate with the Sensors, Perception, and Validation teams to evaluate and iteratively improve the fidelity of sensor simulation
Help create agentic AI systems capable of generating novel scenarios for validating the safety and performance of Zoox's autonomous driving technology
Leverage your software engineering and machine learning skills to gather data, fine-tune existing diffusion / foundation models, add new capabilities to our LLM agent, and evaluate success against customer requirements
Help build scenario generation and analysis tools used to evaluate robot behavior via simulation
Leverage your software engineering and machine learning skills to recreate high-fidelity simulation scenarios from recorded real-world driving data
